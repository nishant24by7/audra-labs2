Capabilities
Advanced Forensic Intelligence

12 proprietary detection engines running in parallel for the most comprehensive image forensics available.

--
Logical Physics Audit
Detects impossible real-world inconsistencies - shadow mismatches, reflection anomalies, object merging errors that AI-generated visuals often break.
--
Biological Anomaly Detection
Scans for non-human facial signatures - unnatural eye-glint patterns, irregular skin pore distribution, absence of micro-vessels in eye whites.
--
Thermal Heatmap Simulation
Simulates biological heat distribution. AI faces appear thermally flat - real human faces have complex blood-flow signatures around eyes and nose.
--
Adversarial Noise Analysis
Detects salt-and-pepper noise and intentional blur used to launder AI artifacts. FFT frequency analysis reveals hidden grid-like AI noise patterns.
--
Steganography Detection
LSB pixel analysis and trailing data scan to detect hidden malware payloads inside images. Turns your tool into a malware defense asset.
--
C2PA Provenance Check
Validates digital content authenticity manifests. Missing C2PA metadata on "official" content is an immediate security red flag.
--
Semantic Consistency Check
Verifies if the image "story" makes sense - weather mismatches, unreal locations, contextual inconsistencies that reveal synthetic fraud.
--
Social Engineering Intent
Beyond "is it AI?" - determines the manipulation intent. Is it designed to create panic, impersonate a CEO, or steal credentials?
--
Batch Redline Reporting
Upload up to 20 images in a single enterprise audit. Generates a security table highlighting every suspicious file in one click.
--
Deepfake AV Alignment
Detects lip-sync gaps in video frames. Even a 2-millisecond delay between speech and lip movement is a definitive deepfake signal.
--
Fraud Risk Scoring
Real-time Low/Medium/High threat scoring with confidence indicators and threat prioritization for fast security decisions.
--
Explainable AI Highlighting
Shows exactly where manipulation exists - pixel-level heatmaps, highlighted suspicious regions, and clear reasoning for transparency.
Green Computing
Built for a Sustainable Future

Audra Labs aligns digital integrity with environmental responsibility - because trust in media and trust in our planet go hand in hand.

Energy Efficiency

Low-Carbon AI Inference

Our serverless architecture on Vercel scales to zero when idle, consuming zero compute when not in use. AI inference is batched and optimized to minimize GPU cycles per analysis - dramatically reducing energy consumption vs. always-on servers.

Misinformation Impact

Fighting Digital Pollution

Every deepfake that spreads causes real-world harm - panic, misinformation, wasted resources in fact-checking. By stopping fake content at the source, Audra Labs reduces the societal energy cost of misinformation and its downstream effects.

Privacy by Design

Zero Data Retention

Images are processed ephemerally and never stored. No data lakes, no unnecessary cloud storage costs, no long-term energy consumption from data retention. Privacy-first design is also green design.

Open Source

Community-Driven Sustainability

Released under MIT License, Audra Labs enables the global community to contribute, audit, and improve the codebase. Shared tools mean less duplicated effort across organizations, and a collectively more efficient ecosystem for digital forensics.
